# Decisions

## 2026-03-19 — Two-phase proposal approach
**Decision**: Phase 1 is a high-level overview to build client confidence. Phase 2 is a deeper dive before committing to final price and timeline.
**Why**: Gives the client a strong signal that we understand the scope without over-committing before we've done the detailed work.

## 2026-03-19 — Branch-per-person creative exploration
**Decision**: Shared discovery data lives on `main`. Each team member branches off to explore their own direction for the proposal tool, then we combine the best ideas.
**Why**: Lets everyone bring their best thinking without stepping on each other's work. The raw data is the shared foundation.

## 2026-03-19 — Document page types, not individual URLs
**Decision**: The front-end audit documents page templates (e.g., "event detail page") rather than every individual URL.
**Why**: Tourism sites can have thousands of URLs but only ~15-20 distinct templates. Documenting each URL would be noise.
