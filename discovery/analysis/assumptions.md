# Assumptions

Assumptions we're making for the Phase 1 proposal. These need to be validated in Phase 2.

## Platform
- The current site runs entirely on Simpleview CMS (no separate microservices or third-party backends beyond Simpleview's own integrations)
- We will have access to Simpleview's data export capabilities for migration
- The rebuild will move off Simpleview entirely (not a Simpleview upgrade)

## Scope
- We are rebuilding both the public-facing website and the content management backend
- Third-party integrations (booking engines, maps, analytics) will be replaced or re-integrated, not rebuilt from scratch
- The new site will maintain SEO parity (URL structure, redirects, meta tags, structured data)

## Timeline
- Target launch: end of 2026
- Phase 1 proposal delivered before committing to final price/timeline
- Phase 2 deep dive happens after Phase 1 client approval

## Team
- 3 developers contributing to this discovery and proposal
- Additional team members may be involved in the actual build
