# Open Questions

Things we need to find out during discovery. Move answers to the relevant doc when resolved.

## Front-end
- How many distinct page types/templates does the site actually have?
- What third-party integrations are embedded (booking widgets, maps, chat, etc.)?
- Are there any authenticated/member-only areas?
- What's the current performance baseline (Lighthouse scores)?

## CMS / Backend
- What Simpleview modules are actively in use?
- How is the CRM integrated with the public site?
- What's the volume of content? (events, listings, blog posts, pages)
- What are the content workflows and approval chains?
- What data export options does Simpleview provide?
- Are there any API integrations we need to preserve?

## SEO
- What's the current organic traffic profile?
- Are there existing redirect chains we need to maintain?
- What structured data (schema.org) is in place?

## Business
- What are the must-have features for launch vs. nice-to-haves?
- Are there any contractual obligations with Simpleview affecting timeline?
- Who are the stakeholders and decision-makers on the client side?
